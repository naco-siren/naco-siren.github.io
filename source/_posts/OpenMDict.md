A free, open-source MDict (.mdx/.mdd) dictionary reader for iPhone and iPad.

## Features

- **Import your own dictionaries** — open `.mdx`/`.mdd` files via the Files app or Share Sheet
- **Preset downloads** — get started instantly with a curated selection of open-source dictionaries
- **Fast lookup** — search across multiple dictionaries at once
- **Bookmarks & history** — keep track of words you've looked up
- **iCloud sync** — optionally sync bookmarks and history across devices
- **Fully offline** — once dictionaries are downloaded, no network needed
- **Open source** — inspect the code, contribute, or fork it
