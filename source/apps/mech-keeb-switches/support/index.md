## FAQ

**Q: Where does my data go?**
A: Everything stays on your device. Favorites and inventory sync via your private iCloud account — the developer cannot access any of it.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**
A: Yes. Once switch images are cached, the app works fully offline.

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: Uninstall the app, or manage your iCloud storage in iOS Settings.
