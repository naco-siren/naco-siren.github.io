## FAQ

**Q: Where does my data go?**
A: Everything stays on your device by default. If you opt in to iCloud sync, data syncs via your private iCloud account — the developer cannot access any of it.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**
A: Yes. Once dictionaries are downloaded, the app works fully offline.

**Q: How do I import my own dictionaries?**
A: Use the Files app or Share Sheet to open `.mdx`/`.mdd` files with OpenMDict.

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: Uninstall the app, or manage your iCloud storage in iOS Settings.
